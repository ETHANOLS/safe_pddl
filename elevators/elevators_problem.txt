A 24-floor building is divided into several service zones. The building contains two types of elevators:

3 fast elevators, each with capacity 6 passengers.
These elevators can travel between major transfer floors across the building.
2 slow elevators per zone, each with capacity 4 passengers.
These elevators operate only inside their assigned local floor area.

Initially, all elevators are located at different floors in the building.

There are multiple passengers waiting on various floors. Each passenger has:

an origin floor
a destination floor

A passenger may need to:

take a slow elevator to a transfer floor
switch to a fast elevator
possibly switch again to another slow elevator
reach the final destination floor

The objective is to generate a plan that transports all passengers to their destinations while minimizing the total travel cost (or total travel time).
